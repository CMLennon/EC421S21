Variable Description
.mono-small[state] State abbreviation
.mono-small[age] The individual's age (in years)
.mono-small[i_urban] Binary indicator for whether home county is 'urban'
.mono-small[i_citizen] Binary indicator for whether the individual is a citizen (naturalized or born.)
.mono-small[i_noenglish] Binary indicator for whether the individual speaks English
.mono-small[i_only_english] Binary indicator for whether the individual speaks ONLY English
.mono-small[i_drive_to_work] Binary indicator for whether the individual drives to work or takes a personal car
.mono-small[i_asian] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified as Asian
.mono-small[i_black] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified as Black
.mono-small[i_indigenous] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified with a group indigenous to North Am.
.mono-small[i_white] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified as White
.mono-small[i_female] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified as Female
.mono-small[i_male] Binary indicator for whether the individual identified as Male
.mono-small[i_grad_college] Binary indicator for whether the individual graduated college
.mono-small[i_grad_highschool] Binary indicator for whether the individual graduated high school
.mono-small[i_married] Binary indicator for whether the individual was married at the time of the sample
.mono-small[i_married_mult] Binary indicator for whether the individual has been married multiple times at the time of the sample
.mono-small[personal_income] Total (annual) personal income (tens of thousands of dollars)
.mono-small[i_health_insurance] Binary indicator for whether the individual has health insurance
.mono-small[i_internet] Binary indicator for whether the individual has access to the internet
.mono-small[time_depart] The time that the individual typically leaves for work (in minutes since midnight)
.mono-small[time_arrive] The time that the individual typically arrives at work (in minutes since midnight)
.mono-small[time_commuting] The length of time that the individual typically travels to work (in minutes)
